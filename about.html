Our Roots

The Jayanti Group's journey began with the visionary Late Shri Jayanti Prasad Jain, who recognized the untapped potential in agri-product business. His legacy birthed a brand that now holds a cherished spot in modern households across the nation. From its humble inception in Alwar, Rajasthan, Jayanti quickly rose to prominence, specializing in crafting premium, hygienic, and delectable Beverages and Snacks.


Today, the Jayanti Group stands tall, surpassing its peers through a customer-centric ethos and an unwavering commitment to quality. Our modern and mature approach ensures that our refreshments are not just treats for the taste buds but a cherished part of your lifestyle. We are proud to serve you with lip-smacking delights that elevate the everyday moments into extraordinary experiences.
